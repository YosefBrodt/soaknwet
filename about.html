Who We Are

A family business
built on craft.

Jax & Jesse's Concrete Cutting & Coring was founded on a simple belief: that quality work and family values go hand in hand. We're a family-run operation serving Ottawa, Gatineau, and the surrounding region — and we bring that same personal accountability to every single project we take on.

Our team has hands-on experience across residential, commercial, and industrial environments. Whether you're enlarging a basement window or managing a full structural demolition on a commercial site, we approach every job with the same precision and care.

We're proud to be working toward union certification — a reflection of our commitment to the trade and to our people.
