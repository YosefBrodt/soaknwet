Who We Are

Ottawa's all-in-one
water company.

Soak N Wet was built around a simple idea: Ottawa shouldn't need four different companies for water delivery, pressure washing, pool care, and plumbing. We bring all of it together under one reliable roof — so you get consistent service, one point of contact, and no runaround.

We're a local Ottawa team with hands-on experience across residential and commercial properties. Whether you need a weekly pool maintenance plan, a one-time driveway pressure wash, or a whole-home water filtration system installed, we show up prepared and we do the job right.

We're proud to serve Ottawa and the surrounding communities — and we treat every client like a neighbour, because many of them are.
