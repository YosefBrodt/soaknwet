The Story Behind
Soak N Wet

Soak N Wet started because we saw a real gap. People needed large volumes of water delivered — pools, wells, job sites — and the options were either slow, unreliable, or both. So we built the operation we'd want to call ourselves.

We run tanker trucks. We know the Ottawa area. We've filled pools in Kanata the same day a homeowner called at 9am. We've rescued families in Carleton Place whose well went dry in August. We've supplied job sites in the city that needed water on a schedule and couldn't afford delays.

We're not trying to be the biggest company in the business. We're trying to be the most reliable one in our market. So far, the phone keeps ringing — and that tells us we're doing something right.

When you call Soak N Wet, you get a straight answer. We'll tell you what we can do, when we can do it, and what it costs. No runaround.
