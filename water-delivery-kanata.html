Water Delivery
in Kanata

We deliver water across Kanata — from Hazeldean and Morgan's Grant to Bridlewood, Beaverbrook, and the new builds around Terry Fox Drive. Whether you need a pool fill for summer, a well top-up, or water on a job site, we get it there fast.

Serving Kanata & Surrounding Areas

Kanata is one of Ottawa's biggest suburbs, and we're on these roads regularly. The newer subdivisions along Terry Fox and Fernbank often need water for landscaping, sod installation, and pool fills on brand-new homes. We also service properties along March Road and the older Kanata neighbourhoods where wells sometimes run dry in the summer months.

  • Construction site supply
