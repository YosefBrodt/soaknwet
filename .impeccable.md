## Design Context

### Users

Homeowners, rural property owners, and contractors in Ottawa and the surrounding region who need water delivered fast—often on tight timelines (pool openings, dry wells, job sites). They discover the site on mobile or desktop, compare trust signals, and convert by phone or quote form.

### Brand Personality

**Reliable · Local · No-nonsense.** The voice is direct and confident, not corporate. Visual tone: premium utility—deep coastal blues, crisp type, water-adjacent cool neutrals, and high-contrast CTAs so booking is never ambiguous.

### Aesthetic Direction

Modern editorial / industrial service: strong display headlines (Barlow Condensed), readable UI body (Space Grotesk), asymmetric layouts with intentional whitespace. Avoid generic “AI gradient” looks; motion is purposeful (staggered reveals, one hero moment), with full `prefers-reduced-motion` support.

### Design Principles

1. **Clarity over decoration** — hierarchy, contrast, and scannable structure first; texture and motion support the story.
2. **One accent family** — cyan/aqua carries brand energy; neutrals stay cool-tinted (not flat gray).
3. **Accessible by default** — body ≥16px, WCAG AA text contrast, focus states, reduced-motion fallbacks.
4. **Cohesive surfaces** — same tokens for nav, sections, and CTAs across Home, Services, About, and Contact.
