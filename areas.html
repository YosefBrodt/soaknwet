Neighbourhoods & corridors

Areas we regularly serve

Every neighbourhood has its own access and scheduling considerations — we plan for all of it.

Core

Central Ottawa & downtown

Centretown, the ByWard Market, Sandy Hill, Glebe, Old Ottawa South — pressure washing, plumbing calls, and water delivery to the heart of the city.

West

Kanata & Stittsville

Residential subdivisions and growing communities — pool openings, water delivery routes, and pressure washing for new builds and established homes.

East

Orleans & east end

Driveway washes, water softener installs, hot tub maintenance — we're on site for homeowners and property managers across the east corridor.

South

Barrhaven & Riverside South

Growing communities with lots of pools and new builds — seasonal pool services, plumbing hookups, and scheduled water delivery.

North

Vanier & Rockcliffe

Older homes with older plumbing — we handle filtration upgrades, pipe repairs, and exterior pressure washing across the north end.
