Areas we regularly serve

Every zone has different access, noise, and scheduling considerations — we plan for all of it.

Core

Central Ottawa & downtown

Centretown, the ByWard Market, Sandy Hill, Glebe, Old Ottawa South — tight sites, occupied buildings, and precision cuts where access is everything.

West

Kanata & Stittsville

Residential subdivisions, retail pads, and light industrial — slab sawing, coring, and selective demolition for new builds and renovations.

East

Orleans & east end

Basement rough-ins, window openings, and driveway work — we’re on site for homeowners and contractors across the east corridor.

South

Barrhaven & Riverside South

Growing communities and commercial strips — controlled cutting and removal with minimal disruption to neighbours.

North

Vanier & Rockcliffe

Older stock and mixed-use — careful demolition and opening work where structure and heritage context matter.
